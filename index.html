Smart Daily Planning

Relationship Intelligence

Never walk into a meeting cold again. Dex automatically builds rich profiles for everyone you interact with — who they are, what you discussed, what they care about.

A Day With Dex

From reactive
to in control

Here’s what a typical day actually looks like.

8:30am

Morning Plan

One command. Five minutes. You get an overnight intel summary, meeting prep for everyone you’re seeing today, and exactly 3 focus items. Heavy meeting day? The system sees that and adjusts automatically.

10:00am

Proactive Support

The system scans recent conversations and surfaces where you can add value. Customer mentioned capability gaps? Dex drafts a message offering your help — before you’re even asked.

2:00pm

After a Call

Meeting ends. Transcript imported. Person pages updated. Three follow-ups extracted. Dependency flagged for the product team. Message drafted with full context, ready to send. Nothing slips through.

Friday

Weekly Synthesis

Themes across your week. Patterns from conversations. Learnings captured silently all week now surfaced with suggestions: “I noticed three improvements we should make. Want me to build them?” Tomorrow’s system is better than today’s.
