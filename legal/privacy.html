Privacy Policy

Last updated 2 June 2026

Dex is a local-first personal knowledge system. The core idea below shapes everything in this policy: your data, and the accounts you connect, stay on your device. We have built Dex so that we — the people behind Dex — see as little of your information as possible.

The short version. Your notes, meetings, files, and the data Dex reads from services you connect (such as Google, Atlassian, or Slack) are stored and processed on your own device. The credentials for those connections are encrypted and held locally in your operating system's secure storage. We do not receive, transmit to our servers, or store that data or those credentials. The only information we process is what you explicitly choose to share with our web services at heydex.ai, plus basic account and product-analytics data described below.

1. Who we are

This policy applies to the Dex application and the website and services at heydex.ai (together, “Dex”, “we”, “us”). For any privacy question or request, contact us at dave@heydex.ai.

2. Our local-first principle

The Dex application runs on your computer. Your knowledge vault — notes, meetings, tasks, people and project pages — is stored as files on your device. Processing that makes Dex useful (search, summarisation, linking) happens locally or, where you have configured an AI model, is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you have chosen under that provider's terms. Except for the limited cases in Section 5, this information is never sent to us.

3. Connected accounts and integrations

Dex lets you connect third-party services (for example Google Workspace, Atlassian, Salesforce, Slack, Linear, and others) so it can work with your information in those services on your behalf.

  • Credentials stay local. When you connect a service, the resulting access token (or API key) is encrypted and stored on your device — in your operating system keychain or an equivalent secure local store. These credentials are not sent to or stored by us.
  • Data stays local. Information Dex reads from a connected service is processed on your device to perform the task you asked for. We do not receive a copy of it.
  • You stay in control. You can disconnect any service at any time, which removes its stored credentials from your device. You can also revoke Dex's access directly in that service's own account settings.

Google API Limited Use

Dex's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. Data obtained from Google is used only on your device to provide features you request, is not transferred to us or to others except as necessary to provide those features at your direction, is not used for advertising, and is not used to train generalised AI/ML models.

4. Information we do not collect

Because of the design above, we do not collect, receive, or store: the contents of your vault; the data Dex reads from your connected services; or the access tokens and API keys for those connections.

5. Information we do process

A small amount of information is processed by our web services at heydex.ai, and only in these situations:

  • Content you publish to heydex.ai. Some features let you publish content to Dex's own infrastructure at heydex.ai — for example, a public profile of how you work, shown at heydex.ai. When you choose to do this, the content you publish is stored by us so it can be displayed there. You decide what, if anything, to publish, and you can remove it.

    This is different from using Dex to help you create content destined for somewhere else — such as drafting a LinkedIn post, an email, or an article. Content meant for a third-party platform is sent to that platform at your direction and under your control: we do not claim ownership of it and we do not store it on our servers.
  • Account and sign-in. If a feature requires an account, we process the identity information needed to authenticate you (such as your name and email, or details from a sign-in provider you choose to use).
  • Product analytics. Dex may collect limited, privacy-respecting usage analytics to improve the product. Analytics can be turned off at any time, and where required we ask for your choice. Analytics never include the contents of your vault or your connected-service data.
  • Support. If you contact us, we process the information you provide so we can help.

6. How we use information

We use the limited information in Section 5 to operate and secure our web services, to provide features you ask for (such as publishing to heydex.ai), to authenticate you, to understand and improve the product in aggregate, and to respond to your requests. We do not sell your personal information.

7. Sharing

We share information only: with service providers who host or operate our web services, under confidentiality obligations; to comply with law or protect rights and safety; or with your direction (for example, content you publish to heydex.ai is shown publicly). We do not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioural advertising.

8. Retention and deletion

Locally stored data lives on your device for as long as you keep it; deleting it in the app or removing a connection removes it from your device. For the limited information we process (Section 5), we keep it for as long as needed to provide the service and then delete or anonymise it. You can ask us to delete information we hold by emailing dave@heydex.ai.

9. Security

Connection credentials are encrypted at rest on your device using your operating system's secure storage. For our web services we use industry-standard measures appropriate to the limited data we process. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but minimising the data we hold is our primary protection.

10. Your rights

Under UK data-protection law (the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018), and depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port the personal information we hold, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. Because most of your data never leaves your device, you already control it directly; for anything we process, contact dave@heydex.ai and we will respond as required by applicable law. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

11. International users

Our web services may process the limited information in Section 5 in countries other than yours. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

12. Children

Dex is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

13. Changes

We may update this policy. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

14. Contact

Questions or requests: dave@heydex.ai.
